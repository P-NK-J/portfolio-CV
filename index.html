The result was a fully designed proof-of-concept delivered across every layer of the experience, from the moment a child launches the app to the moment a parent sees their report card.   • User Flow — Mapped the full app experience from launch through to report card generation, covering every screen state and transition for both child and parent.

  • Level Design — Designed user-based progressive levels that adapt difficulty to the learner — games that feel like play, not testing.
  • Main Character & Customisation — Created the lead character with a full customisation system: hair styles, glasses, shirts, pants, shoes, and accessories — giving children ownership of their avatar and deepening engagement.
  • Reward System — Designed a contextual reward framework that delivers positive reinforcement at the right moments — completing a level, earning a badge, unlocking a new look — keeping motivation high without over-rewarding.
  • Game Animations — Animated characters, scene transitions, and interactive effects across all game states — idle, playing, winning, and celebrating.
  • Dialogue, Audio & SFX — Scripted character dialogues and directed audio and sound effects to match each learning moment and emotional beat.
